# Checkout Shipping Method

Starter app scaffolding for out-of-process shipping carrier setup and the `shipping-methods`
out-of-process shipping rates webhook, built as an independent Adobe Commerce App Management app.

See [Shipping in Checkout Starter Kit](https://developer.adobe.com/commerce/extensibility/starter-kit/checkout/shipping-install)
for the business context, and the [App Management docs](https://developer.adobe.com/commerce/extensibility/app-management/)
for install, build/deploy, and association.
